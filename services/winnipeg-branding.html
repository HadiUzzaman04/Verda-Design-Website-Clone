Logo Design & Branding

We offer branding services in Winnipeg such as logo design, brand identities and full service brand packages. While you are having a website created it can be a great time to refresh or launch a new brand.

Logo Design

We design a memorable logo design by incorporating the values and your brand’s philosophy into your logo. Having an original and unique logo design will set you apart from your competitors. When we develop your logo, we ensure that it translates easily to any form of advertising.

Branding Packages

Branding packages are a great option whether you have a new or established brand. We offer affordable brand packages that will address all of your branding needs. A polished and professional brand is extremely beneficial for long-term success and customer recognition.

Graphic Standards Manual

Ensure your brand identity remains intact and consistent with a graphic standard manual. This manual is a guideline for the usage of the brand we craft for you. This service is extremely important if your company has an internal designer and you wish to maintain the integrity​ of your brand.

Typographic Logos

We adhere to the highest standards in brand development, typography and design. If you are looking to have a typographic logo or wordmark we can craft an eloquent design for you. We give you the full branding experience and develop a branding solution that encompasses your brand.​

Corporate Re-Branding

As your company grows and evolves your brand will need to as well. We ensure longevity and maintain brand recognition through thoughtful brand management. We ensure your brand is consistent with a unique look and feel, typefaces, colour palettes, and has a clear brand message.
